Shibu Warrier


Shibu is a post graduate in HR from XLRI Jamshedpur, with 19 years of experience.

He is currently a Director in Growth Spurt. Before treading on the entrepreneurial path, his last role was with the L&T Financial Services Group, where he was heading HR for its two asset management companies, along with the Organization Development and Performance Management portfolios for the group. He has been exposed to areas like M&A integration, setting up a new performance management system, ESOP framework, and creation of a common grade framework for the group, apart from business HR partnering.

He was also associated with the L&T group and has rich experience handling areas like senior level hiring, campus recruitments for engineers and MBAs, and also performance management for the L&T group.

He started his career as a commissioned officer of the Indian Army, where he did two tenures in the high altitude areas and counter insurgency zones. He had the privilege of being part of Operation Vijay at Kargil.

Apart from facilitating workshops, I also enjoy creating end to end solutions and have extensive experience in assessing training needs, designing, developing and implementing learning interventions for improving organisational health. I am an accredited MBTI practitioner and actively use it in my coaching assignments.

Currently, as Director- Growth Spurt, he consults in the OD space and has worked on assignments in areas like Critical resource identification, Employee engagement surveys and action planning, Manpower Planning, and HR dashboards.
